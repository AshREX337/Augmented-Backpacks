**Backpacked** is a great mod by MrCrayfish but I always felt that it needed more Augments because theres never such a thing as too many.
So I took it upon myself to add some of those useful ones that you see in other Backpack mods while still keeping it balanced to MrCrayfish's style.

If you have any issue or have an idea for a new augment to add, leave a comment!
Currently only available for 1.21.1 NeoForge (I may port to others but it will take a while)

**Gridlock**

![Craft](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AshREX337/Augmented-Backpacks/main/src/main/resources/assets/augmented/craft.png)

Gridlock is a powerful new augment that puts a crafting table directly in your backpack. What's better is that it doesn't require a grid! Simply search up the item you need to craft and it will craft it with items in your inventory (currently not working with items in backpack). 

Works very well on large modpacks with hundreds of crafting recipes too!

---

**Switchup**

![Mine](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AshREX337/Augmented-Backpacks/main/src/main/resources/assets/augmented/mine.png)

Mining dirt with a shovel then suddenly hit stone? Need to chop wood but don't have an axe in your hotbar? Don't worry, the Switchup augment has your back. Switchup allows for you to switch the tool in your main hand with the correct one for the job! As long as a valid tool is found in the backpack (any axe, pickaxe or shovel) it will be swapped into your hand when you need it. 

For an extra bonus, Switchup only swaps tools that way your weapon never leaves your hands.

---

**Gutquench**

![Eat](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AshREX337/Augmented-Backpacks/main/src/main/resources/assets/augmented/eat.png)

Oh this is probably my favorite one. Gutquench (yes cool name I know) automatically consumes any food in your inventory when you're hungry. That seems pretty ordinary right? But Gunquench's speciality is how it determines what food to give you. In the Augment's settings you can pick between prioritizing the recovery of Hunger points (the meat icons) or Saturation (the amount of time before your hunger starts to drain). It also will only consume food if you are as hungry as the amount of hunger points/saturation the food item gives. 

No more wasting time eating on adventures, just load up your backpack with food and off you go.

---

**Savestate**

![Save](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AshREX337/Augmented-Backpacks/main/src/main/resources/assets/augmented/save.png)

Savestate is perfect for those long expeditions where its just you, your backpack, and the dangerous wilderness. Savestate will keep a record of your current xp up till the point you die. It can hold a maximum of 30 levels, which can be increased to 60 if Recall is also equipped. After death, requipping the backpack will give you back the amount of levels you had on death. Now, dying is less of a problem for losing those precious experience points!
